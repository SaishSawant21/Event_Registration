Tech Fiesta

Tech Fiesta, organized by Goa University and co-sponsored by CSI, is an annual IT college festival. It provides a platform for interaction among all computer science students of Goa, and gives them an opportunity to demonstrate their IT skills and also enhance their professional awareness. The first Tech Fiesta meet was held in January 1996. It comprises of various managerial and technical events such as Oratorio, Strategist, Ad-Glib, Quiz, Specialist, Software Contest, Designer, EXE, Word-Wizard and Collectors Clue. Four students who gain the maximum points are then quizzed on various topics by a judging panel to win the title of Systems Analyst.
